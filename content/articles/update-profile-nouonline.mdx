## Quick Answer

To update your NOUN profile:

1. Log into portal
2. Go to **"My Profile"** (top right)
3. Click **"Edit Profile"**
4. Update information (address, phone, email, etc.)
5. Click **"Save Changes"**
6. Portal saves changes immediately

Most changes take effect instantly. Some (like email) may require verification.

---


## What This Guide Covers

This guide explains what profile details students should verify, which changes require care, and when to contact official support instead of guessing.


## What You Can Update

- **Contact Information:** Phone, email
- **Address:** Current address, permanent address
- **Personal Details:** Emergency contact
- **Study Centre:** Location preference
- **Program:** Usually cannot change (contact department if needed)

---

## Step-by-Step Profile Update

### Step 1: Log In

1. Go to the official NOUN student portal
2. Log in with Student ID and password

### Step 2: Access Profile

1. Click your **name** in top right corner
2. Select **"My Profile"** or **"Profile Settings"**
3. You'll see your profile page

### Step 4: Update Information

Edit fields as needed:

**Phone Number:**
- Clear old number
- Enter new number with country code (+234 for Nigeria)
- Format: +234 XXX XXX XXXX

**Email Address:**
- Clear old email
- Enter new email
- Email must be active (verification may be sent)

**Address:**
- Update street address
- Update city/state
- Update postal code

**Emergency Contact:**
- Name
- Phone number
- Relationship

### Step 5: Save Changes

1. Review all changes
2. Click **"Save Changes"** or **"Update Profile"**
3. You'll see success message
4. Changes saved immediately

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: "Can't change email address"

Email change might require verification. Check:
1. Did you receive verification email?
2. Did you click verification link?
3. If not, resend verification from settings

### Issue: "Edit button not showing"

Try:
1. Refresh page
2. Clear cache
3. Try different browser
4. Contact support

### Issue: "Can't change program/student ID"

These usually can't be self-edited. Contact your department.

## Important Notes

- Update your address when it changes.
- Keep your phone number current so your study centre can reach you if needed.
- Use an email address you actually check.
- Review your emergency contact details from time to time.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: Can I change my name on profile?

Usually no through portal. Contact your department for name change.

### Q2: Do I need to update profile every semester?

No, only if information changed.

### Q3: Can I have multiple email addresses on my account?

No, usually just one active email.

### Q4: What if I moved to new address?

Update in portal to make sure mail comes to new address.

## Requirements Before You Start

Before you act, prepare your matriculation number or application record, the current academic session, the relevant course code or payment reference, and a safe place to save receipts or screenshots. Do not share passwords, one-time codes, card details, or unrestricted portal access with anyone offering help.

## Common Mistakes

- Relying on another student's screenshot as your own official record.
- Paying, registering, or updating a record before checking the current session and details.
- Closing the page without saving the reference or final confirmation.
- Sharing portal credentials with a helper or agent.
- Repeating a failed-looking action before confirming whether the first attempt succeeded.

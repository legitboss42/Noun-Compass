## Quick Answer

The Phase 7.3 audit found **three Lagos general centres strong enough to treat as verified**:

- `Lagos Study Centre`
- `Mccarthy Study Centre Lagos`

The audit also found `Mushin Study Centre Lagos` as **partially verified**, while some other Lagos-linked or Lagos-themed centre rows stayed **unverified**. ## Partially Verified Lagos Listing

### Mushin Study Centre Lagos

This row was treated as `PARTIALLY VERIFIED`.

That means:

- there was enough signal to keep it in the Lagos conversation
- the audit did not support publishing it as settled location fact at the same level as Lagos Study Centre, Mccarthy, or Badagry

If Mushin is the centre closest to you, do not assume it is unusable. Just verify it again before travelling and do not copy a third-party address as final.

## Safer Lagos Verification Workflow

### Step 1: Start with the verified-centre list

Use this page and the [national verified list](/articles/full-list-of-verified-noun-study-centres-in-nigeria), not a copied social-media post.

### Step 2: Match the centre to your task

If the issue is admission, take the [admission guide](/articles/how-to-apply-for-noun-admission) with you. If it is results, start with the [results guide](/articles/how-to-check-noun-results). If it is a general portal issue, also prepare the [support ticket guide](/articles/noun-support-ticket-guide).

### Step 3: Reconfirm before travelling

Even verified rows can change in their service arrangement. A verified centre name is not a promise that every office function still works the same way today.

### Step 4: Keep your visit focused

Travel with:

- the exact issue written clearly
- the supporting reference or receipt
- the article or workflow page relevant to your problem

### Step 5: Record the next instruction

If you are redirected to another Lagos centre, support page, or results workflow, keep the note.

## Quick Answer

The 2026 NOUN portal dashboard includes:
- **Course Dashboard:** View enrolled courses and materials
- **Payments:** Pay fees and check balance
- **Results:** View grades and transcripts
- **My Profile:** Update personal information
- **Study Materials:** Download course resources
- **Messages:** Communicate with advisors
- **Support:** Submit tickets and get help

Everything is organized in the left sidebar menu or top navigation.

## What This Guide Covers

This guide walks through the main dashboard features, where to find each function, how to reach materials, results, payments, and profile settings, plus a few simple navigation tips.

## Finding What You Need

### Find Your Courses

1. Click **"Courses"** in left menu
2. View all enrolled courses
3. Click course name to open course materials
4. See course schedule, lecturer info, assignments

### Check Your Results

1. Click **"Results"** in left menu
2. Select semester
3. View grades for all courses
4. Download transcript (if available)

### Manage Your Payments

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: How do I contact my lecturer?

Go to Courses, open the course, then look for the lecturer contact option or the email listed on the page.

### Q2: Where do I see my financial statement?

Open Payments, then Financial Statement, and download the PDF if that option is available.

### Q3: How do I register for courses?

During the registration period, open Courses, choose Register for Courses, select the right courses, and continue to payment if required.

### Q4: Can I change my email in profile?

Yes, My Profile → Edit → Email → Save. But you might need identity verification.

## Requirements Before You Start

Before you act, prepare your matriculation number or application record, the current academic session, the relevant course code or payment reference, and a safe place to save receipts or screenshots. Do not share passwords, one-time codes, card details, or unrestricted portal access with anyone offering help.

## Step-by-Step Verification Process

1. Start from an official NOUN page or a portal link you independently verified.
2. Confirm the session, semester, programme, level, course code, or payment reference that applies to the task.
3. Read the page carefully before clicking a button that submits, pays, updates, or registers anything.
4. Save the final receipt, slip, acknowledgement, or screen record where available.
5. If the outcome is unclear, stop and prepare one clear support report instead of repeating the action.

## Common Mistakes

- Relying on another student's screenshot as your own official record.
- Paying, registering, or updating a record before checking the current session and details.
- Closing the page without saving the reference or final confirmation.
- Sharing portal credentials with a helper or agent.
- Repeating a failed-looking action before confirming whether the first attempt succeeded.

## Troubleshooting

If the portal does not show what you expected, first confirm that you are on the right page and that your session has not expired. Compare the live screen with your saved evidence. If a payment, registration, profile, or course record still looks wrong, gather the exact date, reference, course code, screenshot, and expected outcome before you contact support.

## Official Sources and Verification Notes

This guide helps you find your way around the dashboard, but final fees, deadlines, course availability, payment status, registration records, and academic decisions still need to be confirmed on official NOUN pages. Portal labels and rules can change after this article is reviewed.

# Final Authority Scorecard

Last updated: 2026-06-20

## Overall Authority Score

**Current Authority Score: 89/100**

This score is slightly stricter than the earlier build-phase estimate of `93/100` because the full audit surfaced:

- 8 orphaned pages
- uneven source specificity
- several thin support pages
- one high-risk older finance article

The site is still strong enough to move into Phase 8, but the audit shows it is not a perfect finish-state.

## Cluster Verdict

The strongest clusters are:

- Registration
- NELFUND
- TMA/eLearn
- Results
- Fees

The weakest clusters are:

- Course Materials
- Support
- Portal as a standalone issue-resolution branch

## Scorecard Verdict

NounCompass is now a real topical authority website for NOUN students, but it still has a refinement backlog. The remaining gaps are no longer about missing major subject branches. They are about consistency, depth at the edges, and making older content match the newer authority standard.

Last updated: 2026-06-19

## Rule For This Plan

This cluster plan is built only from workflows and pain points directly observed in the live portal session. Unverified topics such as transcript requests and explicit carryover workflows are still excluded.

## Recommended Pillar Page

### Pillar

`How To Check NOUN Results Using My Progress, The Support Portal, And The Statement Of Result Page`

Why this is the right pillar:
- `My progress` is the confirmed results entry point
- students are likely to search for `results`, not `my progress`
- the live portal itself points students toward Support/e-Ticketing when more help is needed
- the support portal exposes the verified `Result statement` route
- the ERP result page shows CGPA, class of degree, failed courses, outstanding credit, and outstanding courses

## Student Journey Structure

Observed and supported journey:

1. Student logs into the NOUN portal
2. Student opens `My progress`
3. Student reviews historical semester rows
4. Student notices missing grades or incomplete detail
5. Student follows support instruction
6. Student opens `Support`
7. Student uses `Drop a complaint (e-Ticketing)`
8. Student logs into support portal
9. Student opens `Result statement`
10. Student checks grades, CGPA, class of degree, and outstanding courses

Related academic-proof journey:

1. Student tries to confirm registration history
2. Student opens `See course registration slip`
3. Student hits current-semester registration blocker if registration is incomplete
4. Student returns to registration workflow before expecting record access

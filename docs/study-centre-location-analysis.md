# NOUN Study Centre Location Analysis

Last updated: 2026-06-19

This location analysis is based on the Phase 7.3 inventory and verification pass. State-level counts are more reliable than fully automated city extraction, so this document uses a state-cluster view first and then highlights the strongest city-level targets manually.

## Largest State Clusters

| State | Centres Found | Why It Matters |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Kano | 16 | Largest cluster in the audited inventory, with strong list intent and multiple centre variants. |
| FCT | 8 | Abuja is one of the clearest high-intent student locations and has stronger official evidence than many other city clusters. |
| Lagos | 7 | Already partly covered on NounCompass, but still needs stronger, verification-backed expansion. |
| Delta | 5 | Useful supporting territory, but not the first city-cluster priority. |
| Ogun | 4 | Abeokuta plus other Ogun-linked centres make this a strong regional cluster. |
| Osun | 4 | Good supporting depth, but weaker than Abuja, Lagos, Kano, and Ogun for immediate authority impact. |
| Kwara | 4 | Useful, though not the first expansion priority. |
| Benue | 4 | Good supporting depth, but verification strength is mixed. |
| Kogi | 4 | Good supporting depth, but better after top clusters are built. |
| Niger | 3 | Moderate opportunity, but not a first-wave cluster. |

## Strongest City-Level Targets

These are the clearest city or metro targets from the current evidence set:

| Location | Confidence | Why It Is A Priority |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Abuja / FCT | High | Multiple FCT-linked centres appeared, and Abuja Model Study Centre was strongly confirmed on official NOUN search. |
| Lagos | High | Existing NounCompass coverage plus several Lagos-linked centre rows make this a clear authority gap to expand. |
| Kano | Medium-High | The state has the highest centre concentration in the inventory, even though many rows are only partially verified. |
| Abeokuta / Ogun | Medium-High | Abeokuta itself surfaced with direct verification, and Ogun has enough surrounding support to justify a regional page. |
| Enugu | Medium | Enugu and Awgu surfaced with usable evidence, making Enugu one of the better non-mega-city support clusters. |
| Benin | Medium | Benin Study Centre was directly verified and is a strong city-intent candidate. |
| Kaduna | Medium | Kaduna has a verified general centre signal, but surrounding special-centre rows are weaker. |

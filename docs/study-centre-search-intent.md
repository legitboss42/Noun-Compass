# NOUN Study Centre Search Intent

Last updated: 2026-06-19

This intent map is based on the verified and partially verified centre inventory, the current NounCompass content gap, and the practical questions students are likely to ask before travelling or choosing a centre.

## Core Search Themes

| Search Theme | Example Queries | Intent Type | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| National directory | `noun study centres list`, `full list of noun study centres in nigeria`, `noun study centre directory` | Navigational + informational | Students need one trusted page that shows where NOUN actually has centres nationwide. |
| Nearest or state-based centre discovery | `noun study centre near me`, `noun study centre in lagos`, `noun study centre in abuja`, `noun study centre in kano` | Local navigational | Students often begin with geography, not programme structure. |
| Address verification | `noun study centre address`, `noun lagos centre address`, `where is noun study centre in abuja` | Informational + local action | Students do not want to travel on outdated map pins or recycled blog posts. |
| Contact verification | `noun study centre contact number`, `noun study centre email`, `how to contact noun study centre` | Support / contact intent | Students want to confirm availability before travelling. |
| Service-specific centre choice | `noun study centre for exams`, `noun study centre for clearance`, `noun study centre for admission issues` | Task intent | Students need the right centre for the right workflow, not just the nearest location. |
| Special / correctional centres | `noun correctional study centre`, `noun special study centre`, `noun prison study centre` | Informational + policy | The source inventory shows these centre types exist, but students need careful, accuracy-first guidance. |
| Centre-selection support | `which noun study centre should i choose`, `best noun study centre in lagos`, `can i change noun study centre` | Decision intent | Students want help choosing or confirming a centre during admission or later transitions. |

## Key Reading of the Map

- The site is already clearly centered on NOUN student workflows, not random educational topics.
- Fees and portal/registration are the strongest authority clusters.
- Results, study centres, GST, and course materials each depend on a single article or section-level surface.
- TMA and NELFUND are the largest authority gaps because they are named in the intended ecosystem but not properly represented in published content.

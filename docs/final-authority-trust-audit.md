# Final Authority Trust Audit

Last updated: 2026-06-20

## Trust Summary

NounCompass has strong site-level trust scaffolding:

- author pages
- editorial policy
- corrections policy
- privacy, terms, disclaimer, copyright, and takedown pages
- article template with author, reviewer, updated date, source note, disclaimer, and related reads

The remaining trust gaps are now mostly article-level consistency issues.

## Trust And E-E-A-T Findings

| Area | Status | Finding |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Author signals | Moderate | The site has author and reviewer identities, but most content still resolves to the same editorial identity rather than a broader subject-matter byline mix |
| Editorial signals | Strong | Policy pages and review language are present and coherent |
| Verification language | Moderate | Newer clusters use explicit verification boundaries well; older articles rely more on generic `verify through official channels` language |
| Update language | Moderate | The template shows updated dates and last-checked boxes, but many articles do not include an in-body last-reviewed or verification section |
| Source specificity | Weak | Many articles still use a generic homepage or broad portal URL as `officialSourceUrl` rather than the most precise supporting source |
| Independence language | Strong | Independent-resource disclaimers are clear and consistent |
| Student trust feel | Moderate | The site feels careful overall, but some older pages still read like generalized advice rather than observed or tightly sourced NOUN guidance |

## AdSense-Relevant Trust Gaps

The main trust gaps that could matter for AdSense or broader quality review are:

- generic source attribution on older articles
- a small set of unsupported or weakly supported finance claims in `noun-installment-payment`
- uneven article quality between newer verified clusters and older listicle-style pages
- orphaned pages that make the authority ecosystem look less intentional than it actually is

## Strong Trust Signals Already Present

- sitewide independent-resource disclaimer
- clear editorial policy
- correction and contact paths
- author and reviewer fields on articles
- source note and last-checked UI on article pages
- strong verification language in results, TMA, study-centre, and NELFUND clusters

## Remaining Trust Gaps

The most important trust gaps left are:

- homepage-level official source URLs used where more exact references exist
- lack of explicit in-body verification sections on many older pages
- weak article-level sourcing specificity in older finance, portal, and exam explainers
- inconsistent experiential tone across the archive

## Trust Verdict

NounCompass has enough trust structure to proceed to Phase 8. The next trust improvement is not adding more policy pages. It is making the older article inventory match the same source-specific, verification-first standard already visible in the newer clusters.

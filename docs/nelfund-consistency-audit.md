# NELFUND Consistency Audit

Last updated: 2026-06-20

## Scope

This audit reviewed finance-related content to find claims that are stronger than the verified NELFUND evidence established in Phase 7.4.

## Overall Verdict

The dedicated NELFUND cluster is internally consistent and respects the verified / partially verified / unverified boundary well. The main consistency risk now sits in older finance content rather than inside the new cluster itself.

## Findings

| Article | Severity | Finding | Why It Is A Problem |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `noun-installment-payment` | High | The NELFUND mention itself is now cautious, but the surrounding article still contains unsupported finance claims about informal payment plans, `<10%` approval odds, hardship funds, scholarships, and outside loan options | Even if not directly a NELFUND claim, it weakens the reliability of the surrounding finance cluster |
| `how-to-pay-noun-school-fees` | Medium | No direct unsupported NELFUND claim, but the page still uses a generic homepage source and does not explicitly frame NELFUND as a separate verified branch | It does not contradict the cluster, but it is looser than the new evidence standard |
| `noun-school-fees-new-students` | Medium | No unsupported NELFUND wording found, but the page is lightly sourced and not strongly connected to the NELFUND branch | Students planning fees may miss the finance-support path entirely |
| `noun-school-fees-returning-students` | Medium | Same pattern as the new-student fee page: no direct unsupported NELFUND claim, but weak crossover into verified student-finance guidance | This creates a softer consistency gap, not a factual contradiction |
| Dedicated NELFUND cluster pages | Low | New pages consistently mark unresolved issues as unverified or partially verified | This is the current site standard and should become the model for older finance content |

## Unsupported Eligibility Claims

No unsupported eligibility claim was found inside the new NELFUND pages. The cluster consistently avoids presenting these as facts:

- postgraduate eligibility
- returning-student eligibility
- new-student timing
- exact universal JAMB regularization requirements

## Unsupported Approval Claims

No unsupported approval guarantee was found inside the new NELFUND cluster. The pages repeatedly avoid claiming:

- guaranteed approval
- guaranteed funding
- guaranteed timing

## Unsupported Regularization Claims

No dedicated NELFUND page crossed the line into presenting unverified regularization rules as settled fact. The new cluster consistently keeps this as partial or unresolved.

## Outdated Finance Guidance

The older finance pages still create the biggest consistency risk because they are more willing to speak in general student-finance terms without the same evidence discipline now used in the NELFUND branch.

The page most in need of authority-standard cleanup later is:

- `noun-installment-payment`

## Consistency Verdict

The NELFUND cluster itself is consistent enough for Phase 8. The remaining issue is not NELFUND misinformation inside the new cluster. It is that some older fee pages still operate at a looser trust standard and should eventually be brought into line with the verified student-finance approach.

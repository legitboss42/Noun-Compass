e-Courseware Instructions

NOUN Prohibits the use of its e-Courseware for; Commercial, Financial purposes other than Educational.

The e-Courseware is a repository of available digital course material of the National Open University that will help you in your course of studies.
You can filter for the course material you want by Faculty, Semester and Level.

To be able to read any of the course materials in this library offline, you must have Adobe acrobat reader (or any other pdf reader) installed on your computer.

  • Download adobe pdf reader here
  • Download foxit pdf reader here

You can also Open and read online with a browser that supports pdfs (chrome, firefox, etc).

  1. To read any of the course materials online, click on the title/link to open that material in your browser.
  2. To download, either use the download/save option that appears after you have opened it in your browser or right-click the title/link and select the “save link as..” option.

If you have any questions regarding the e-Courseware library, please contact the Head Web unit by emailing: nounweb@noun.edu.ng

Please Note: Emails with respect to e-Courseware issues should have the subject “e-Courseware”.

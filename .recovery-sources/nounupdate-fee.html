Important Information

  • All students must pay Tertiary Institutions Social Health Insurance Programme (TISHIP) of ₦5,000 which is already added to your semester registration fee.
  • Semester fee total for 200/300 level direct entry students in their first semester is ₦65,250, not the value shown on some older schedules.
  • The standard semester fee for all returning students with matric number from NOU23 whose session started from 2023 2nd semester has been updated to ₦35,250.
  • Those whose matric number are 2023 first semester downward are expected to pay ₦26,750.
  • The maximum credit units for exam registration per semester is 24.
  • All core (compulsory) courses must be registered. At least one elective course is required.
  • This Fee Checker provides an estimate. The final, official fee is what is displayed on the nouonline.nou.edu.ng portal during your registration.

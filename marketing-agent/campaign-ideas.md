## Best current campaign clusters

- Push fee-related articles with proven search demand

### Cluster 3: Portal and registration recovery

- Solve common stuck points
- Strong fit for quick posts, threads, and carousel tips

### Cluster 4: TMA and exam help

- Best for short-form video and saveable educational posts
- Good trust builder even when no direct sale happens

# Post Templates

## Admission service post

- Problem: applicant confusion, missing steps, document uncertainty
- Solution: explain the next step in simple language
- CTA: Need help with NOUN admission? Contact NounCompass for guidance.
- Link: /admission or /contact
- Safety line: NounCompass is an independent student-help platform and is not the official NOUN website.
- Best use: Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp Business, LinkedIn

## Website traffic post

- Problem: student needs a fast answer now
- Solution: point to a useful guide on the site
- CTA: Read the full guide on NounCompass
- Link: a specific article or landing page
- Best use: Facebook, X, Pinterest, LinkedIn
- Ideal topics: Remita, fees, portal fixes, results, course materials

## Helpful educational post

- Problem: student is stuck on a process
- Solution: give 3 clear steps or one short checklist
- CTA: Save this for later
- Link: /student-guides or a supporting article
- Best use: carousel, thread, or short post

## FAQ post

- Problem: a common question that repeats often
- Solution: answer in one sentence, then expand slightly
- CTA: Drop your question in the comments
- Link: relevant guide or /contact
- Trust note: use this format for "Are you official?" and similar clarification questions

## Engagement post

- Problem: the audience needs a low-friction interaction
- Solution: ask a useful either-or question or a quick poll
- CTA: Comment with your answer
- Best use: Facebook, Instagram Stories, X, Threads

## Pinterest evergreen pin

- Problem: search-friendly student need
- Solution: one promise, one benefit, one clear path
- CTA: Open the guide
- Best use: vertical pin with simple headline

## Short video idea

- Hook: one student pain point in the first 2 seconds
- Body: one process, one mistake to avoid, one next step
- CTA: Watch the full guide or check the link in bio
- Best use: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts

## Required output fields for every daily post

- Platform
- Student problem
- Caption
- Image brief
- CTA
- Hashtags
- Link target
- Best posting format
- Reason for the post
- Expected goal
